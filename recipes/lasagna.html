Lasagna Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps to Make Lasagna

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, basil, salt, black pepper, oregano, and red pepper flakes. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of the ricotta mixture. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
