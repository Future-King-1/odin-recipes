Beef Steak Ingredients

  • 2 beef steaks (ribeye, sirloin, or filet mignon)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary or thyme
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce (optional for extra flavor)

Steps to Cook Beef Steak

  1. Remove the steak from the fridge and let it come to room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Pat the steak dry with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.
  3. Rub both sides of the steak with olive oil, salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  4. Heat a pan or skillet over high heat until very hot.
  5. Add the steak to the pan and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  6. Reduce heat to medium and add butter, garlic, and rosemary/thyme.
  7. Baste the steak with melted butter by tilting the pan and spooning it over the steak.
  8. Cook to desired doneness (125°F for rare, 135°F for medium-rare, 145°F for medium).
  9. Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes!
